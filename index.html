We are OTD (OnTimeDelivery).

OTD began as a platform to address inefficiencies in the last mile logistics sector and improve the way items are carried around cities, allowing lakhs of firms to move anything on-demand. We've expanded exponentially since then, significantly improving corporate efficiency, producing enormous value for our partner-drivers, and bringing enjoyment to a growing list of five cities. But our adventure is only getting started; our objective is to create the world's greatest end-to-end logistics platform and revolutionise the transportation logistics sector.

OTD is dedicated to improve the quality of life for our partner drivers. Our partners benefit from flexible working hours, greater earnings, and a healthy work-life balance, as well as extra perks like lower insurance and fuel costs. We've also launched a number of community development projects, such as higher education grants and scholarships for our partners' children, specialised health care help, and automobile leasing aid.
